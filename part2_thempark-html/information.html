Security and behaviour rules

Please follow these rules. It is for your safety and secures an enjoyable stay in Fantastic Ride World.

  • Follow any time the instructions of our staff.
  • Fasten the seat belts on the wildes rides.
  • Roller coaster minimum age: 11 years.
  • Don't eat on the rides, but only in the restaurant.
  • If you see the automatic camera on the ride: please smile.
